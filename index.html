Privacy Policy

Last updated — June 2025

1. Who We Are

Top_trend ("we", "us", "our") is a fashion content platform that curates and publishes minimalist fashion content on Pinterest and other social media channels. We operate as a content creator and affiliate marketing business focused on the US market.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from visitors to this website. Our platform is purely informational. When you interact with our Pinterest content or purchase products through our affiliate links, data collection is governed by Pinterest's and the retailer's respective privacy policies.

3. Affiliate Links

Some links shared through our Pinterest boards are affiliate links. This means we may earn a small commission if you purchase through these links — at no additional cost to you. We only share products that genuinely align with our aesthetic and quality standards.

4. Pinterest API Usage

Top_trend uses the Pinterest API to publish content programmatically to our own Pinterest boards. Our API usage is strictly limited to managing our own account. We do not collect, store, or process any data belonging to other Pinterest users. Specifically, we use the API to:

  • Publish pins to our own boards
  • Read our own board information
  • Manage our own content calendar and publishing schedule

5. Cookies

This website does not use cookies or any tracking technologies of any kind.

6. Third-Party Services

Our content links to third-party platforms including Pinterest and SHEIN. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external platforms and encourage you to review their privacy policies directly.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to ensure the integrity of our platform. No sensitive user data is collected or stored by Top_trend.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be published on this page with a revised date at the top.

9. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our Pinterest profile.
